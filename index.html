Engagement Story

We started in San Francisco with a camper-van, some pots and pans and a ten-day plan to celebrate our fifth anniversary along the rugged Californian coast. With Kate charting our course, and myself helming the wheel of our trusted mini-van “Arlo,” we adventured our way up the winding cliffs of the Pacific Coast Highway until we hit the famous Avenue of the Giants, in Humboldt Redwoods State Park. What happened next could best be described as the end of one great adventure, and the beginning of one far, far greater. There, among trees that predated the new world, Kate and I became engaged on Sept. 29th, 2015. Back at the campsite, we celebrated the best way we knew how: a dinner of campfire chili-dogs and champagne beneath the moonlight. Now, almost exactly a year later, we cannot wait to embark on this next great adventure with you in the tree-lined cathedrals of the Morton Arboretum!

-Paul
